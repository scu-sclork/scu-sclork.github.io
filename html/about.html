About


Santa Clara University's Laptop Orchestra SCLOrk is an ensemble of upto 16 players dedicated to the creation and performance of live electronic music.

Created and directed by Dr. Bruno Ruviaro, SCLOrk draws on music composition, sound design, and programming for performance art.

While SCLOrk is offered as MUSC 157/57 at SCU every winter, Chamber SCLOrk (subgroup within SCLOrk) works year-round to create new pieces.

In 2019, SCLOrk performed at Dolby Headquarters in San Francisco. In 2018, Chamber SCLOrk performed outdoors in downtown San Jose's SoFA district. In 2017, Chamber SCLOrk performed at Anno Domini in San Jose with Dutch sound poet and electronic musician Jaap Blonk.

Let's make some noise, together!

Tanya Sonker, Santa Clara University (2019-2020)
